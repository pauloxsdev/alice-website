The Laws of Absurdity

⏳
Rule #1: Time is a Person
Time is a real person! If you are bad to him, he will stop your clock forever.
🎭
Rule #2: Identity is Fluid
You change all the time. Alice says she changed many times in just one day.
🐾
Rule #3: Animals have Authority
Animals are the bosses here. Caterpillars give advice and cats teach you things.

Behind the Illusions

The Real Alice 👧
Alice was a real person! The author wrote this story in 1862 to make a 10-year-old girl named Alice laugh.
Hidden Math 📐
The author was a math teacher. Because of this, the book has many secret math games and puzzles.
Toxic Hats 🧪
In the past, people who made hats really got sick and went crazy because of the bad chemicals they used.

Alice
Deep Literary Analysis
Deep Lore: Alice represents a child trying to understand adult rules. When she changes size, it shows how confusing it is to grow up.
×
The Cheshire Cat
Deep Literary Analysis
Deep Lore: The author used a popular joke to create this cat. The cat shows us that reality changes depending on how you look at things.
×
The Mad Hatter
Deep Literary Analysis
Deep Lore: The tag on his hat shows its price. He is stuck in a tea party because he made 'Time' angry. Now, his clock is stopped at 6:00 PM forever.
×
The Queen of Hearts
Deep Literary Analysis
Deep Lore: She represents angry bosses. Even though she always shouts 'Off with their heads!', no one actually dies in the book because the King secretly saves them.
